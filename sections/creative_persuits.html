Times of Twitter is a (currently in development) tool that automates your Twitter feed into a personalized, daily newspaper. It extracts tweets, summarizes them using AI, and sends the summarized content in a clean, newspaper-style PDF to your email inbox every morning.

Key features include:

  • Twitter Feed Extraction: Automatically pulls relevant tweets from your home timeline using the Twitter API.
  • AI-Powered Summarization: Summarizes tweets into concise, readable sections, condensing long threads into key takeaways.
  • PDF Newspaper Format: Formats the summarized tweets into a structured, newspaper-style PDF for easy reading.
  • Automated Email Delivery: Sends the PDF to your email inbox each morning, ensuring you get your daily digest effortlessly.

This project combines automation, AI, and daily content consumption, transforming your Twitter experience into a personalized, easily digestible format.

bitcoin_utils is my personal suite of tools and utilities tailored for Bitcoin-related projects. The repository focuses on providing data science and cryptographic utilities to optimize Bitcoin transactions, manage seed phrases, and fetch real-time Bitcoin prices.

Key features include:

  • Dynamic Bitcoin Transaction Fee Estimator: Optimizes Bitcoin transaction costs based on real-time mempool data, allowing for priority-based fee calculation.
  • BIP-39 Seed Phrase Validator: Implements a manual validation method for BIP-39 seed phrases, ensuring both word validity and checksum accuracy.
  • Private Key Generator: Generates secure private keys from BIP-39 seed phrases with optional passphrase support.
  • Live Bitcoin Price Fetcher: Retrieves real-time Bitcoin prices across multiple fiat currencies.

This is my personal utility project to enhance my understanding of Blockchain development. It showcases my ability to work with Python and cryptographic libraries.
