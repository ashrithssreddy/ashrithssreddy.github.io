About Me

Hi there! I'm Ash, a curious explorer of technology, data, and the world around me. Some of my friends call me "Florida Man", after the Netflix series. I love beaches, books, and the satisfaction of bringing order to chaos. While I’m more of a quiet observer, I deeply value my connections and am always here for those around me. I believe in continuous growth, embracing challenges, and living life with patience, curiosity, and a systematic approach.

My journey has taken me from a tranquil bucolic villages of South India, to the hustle-and-bustle of Hollywood Hills, and now to tech-hubs of Austin. Along the way, I've explored the soul of emerging economies and the vibrance of the world's most dynamic cities. Each place taught me something new, reminding me of my privilege to adapt, learn, and grow through such diverse experiences.
